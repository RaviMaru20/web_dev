Park Information

Booking tickets

Booking tickets at the Ghibli Park is super easy!

Simply follow the steps below:

  1. Choose your date and time of arrival.
  2. Choose your number and type of tickets. The following tickets types are available:
    • Adult - $10
    • Child - $5
    • Senior - $7

  3. Choose your booking method.

    The following booking methods are possible:

    • Online booking:

      You can book tickets online by filling out the form on the right.

    • App booking:

      App users may be elligible for special discounts!

      Download our apps by searching "The Ghibli Park" in your app store!

    • In-person booking

      You can book tickets in person by calling the park office.

How to get there

Find ways to get to Ghibli Park

  • Hotel shuttle: guests may use the shuttle buses for free.
  • By car: cars and motorcycles are allowed to use the car park. Please follow the directions.
  • By bus: take line 13 from Nagoya train station and stop at Chihiro bus stop.
  • By taxi: a taxi drop-off is located near the main entrance.
  • By subway: from Nagoya Central, take line 7 and stop at the Mononoke terminus.
  • From the airport: numerous ways are available to access our theme park.

Frequently asked questions

Covid 19 regulations:

Should I wear a mask during my visit?

Wearing a mask is recommended from the age of 11 in the aisles and compulsory in the rest of the park.

How are park staff equipped?

Wearing a mask is mandatory for park staff.

What are the cleaning and implementation measures indicated?

Contact areas such as queue barriers, restaurant tables and payment terminals are cleaned at an appropriate frequency in accordance with the Health Security Plan validated by the prefecture.

Is the Health Pass mandatory?

The Sanitary Pass is compulsory from 12 years and two months to access the park.

The Sanitary Pass is a printed or digital document that is justified:

Either by a vaccination certificate (complete schedule), Either by an RT-PCR or antigenic test certificate of less than 48 hours, Either by a Covid-19 recovery certificate: RT-PCR or positive antigen test dating back more than 11 days and less than 6 months.

Ticketing

Do you have to print the e-tickets or is it enough to show the barcode on your phone?

Yes, it is preferable to print your tickets.

Are all the attractions and shows included in the admission ticket?

Yes, with your admission ticket you can enjoy all our attractions and shows.

Can we show up with tickets (or e-tickets) in someone else's name?

Yes, it is possible to show up at the park with tickets in someone else's name if it is a group booking or a gift from that other person.

Is it possible to buy tickets on site?

It is best to book your entrance tickets directly online to avoid groupings at the cash desks. You will also save 100 yen per ticket!

Can an adult ticket be used for a child?

Yes, it is possible to use an adult ticket for a child.

How much does it take to get a group rate?

The group rate is valid from 20 people. In this case, the reservation is obligatory. Just call +1(888)- 154-7854 or send an email to team.ghibli@j.com.

Services

Can we rent strollers in the park?

Yes, the park offers a stroller rental service.

Can we find a baby space?

Yes, we have a baby relay located at the entrance near the picnic area. This consists of a microwave, high chairs, a space for changing babies and a sink.

Are lockers available at the park?

Yes, the park offers lockers near the ticketing area.

Are there wheelchair rentals in the park?

Yes, the park offers a wheelchair rental service.

Accessibility

Can we access the park with animals?

No, only guide dogs and assistance dogs accompanying people with disabilities are allowed.

Is it possible to come in and out as many times as we want?

Yes, leaving the park is allowed. For this, a stamp is available at the entrance to the park, you just have to ask the operator on site. You may be asked for your entry ticket.

Attractions

Are the attractions accessible to everyone?

No, all attractions are subject to size restrictions and health conditions.

Are there attractions for 2-year-olds?

Accessibility to attractions depends on the size of the child. Two thirds of our attractions are accessible from 90cm

Do children have to be accompanied by an adult on the attractions?

A child whose size requires accompaniment must be accompanied by an adult.

Do the attractions operate in bad weather?

For safety measures, some attractions may be temporarily stopped in the event of inappropriate weather (rain, storm, violent wind, hail, etc.).

Picnic

Can we picnic in the park?

Yes, the park offers a covered area for picnics as well as picnic tables.
